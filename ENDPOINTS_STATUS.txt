  Observed: Works, but may return mixed blank/non-blank names

- GET https://pd.{shard}.a.pvp.net/match-history/v1/history/{puuid}?startIndex={i}&endIndex={j}
  Type: Post-match history
  Use: recent completed matches list
  Observed: Works

- GET https://pd.{shard}.a.pvp.net/match-details/v1/matches/{matchId}
  Type: Post-match (per docs/current behavior)

  Type: Intended live in older community references
